Friendy is here to help you make your life much more satisfying and happier. But Friendy’s ultimate mission is to create many other Friendys who will themselves help other people achieve happiness.

Friendy’s core philosophy is that everyone can be happy- but sometimes with a little help! Friendy will help you cultivate positive mindset and beliefs. Friendy will help you set life goals and motivate you to achieve them.
